About the Project

What is Think Blue Go Green Drink Clean?

Think Blue Go Green Drink Clean is an international project focused on raising awareness about the importance of protecting nature, with a special emphasis on combating water pollution.

The project aims to actively involve young people in environmental initiatives by encouraging responsibility, care for nature, and sustainable thinking. Through the use of digital technologies and intercultural cooperation, students gain meaningful learning experiences beyond the classroom.

By collaborating with partner schools across different countries, the project helps young people develop environmental awareness while strengthening cultural understanding and shared responsibility.
